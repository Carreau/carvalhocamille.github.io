## Research Focus

### Primary Research Areas
1. **Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)** with applications to wave phenomena
2. **Computational Electromagnetics** - numerical methods for electromagnetic wave propagation
3. **Plasmonics and Metamaterials** - mathematical modeling of plasmonic structures
4. **Boundary Integral Methods** - close evaluation problems and layer potentials
5. **Finite Element Methods** - high-order accurate methods for interface problems
6. **Numerical Analysis** - asymptotic methods, spectral theory, scattering theory
7. **Wave Propagation** - scattering, resonances, and limiting amplitude principles

### Specific Contributions

**Boundary Integral Equations:**
- Developed asymptotic approximations for close evaluation of layer potentials
- Created QPAX (Quadrature by Parity Asymptotic eXpansions) method for high aspect ratio particles
- Addressed fundamental challenges in accurate near-field computation

**Plasmonic Structures:**
- Analyzed resonances and instabilities in metamaterial cavities
- Studied corner singularities in plasmonic structures
- Developed FEM methods capturing near-fields in plasmonic devices

**Sign-Changing Coefficients:**
- Investigated eigenvalue problems with sign-changing coefficients (modeling metamaterials)
- Analyzed mesh requirements for finite element approximation
- Studied Perfectly Matched Layers (PMLs) at corners
